# NIVAAS Figma Links

## Hi-Fi Interactive Prototype

**[Open NIVAAS Prototype in Figma](https://www.figma.com/design/CqKJ35W9IJGI1nEKeTRJRG/MAIN?node-id=0-1&p=f)**

The main Figma file contains the complete high-fidelity design including:

- Welcome & onboarding screens
- Role selection (Nodal Officer, General Citizen, Slum Residents)
- Login, signup, and authentication flow
- Citizen / Slum Dweller dashboard and flows
- Nodal Officer / Government dashboards
- GIS mapping, drone survey, AI risk map interfaces
- Welfare schemes, emergency services, grievance tracking

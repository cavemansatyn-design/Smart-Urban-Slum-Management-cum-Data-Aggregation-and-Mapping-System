# NIVAAS User Guide

## Introduction

This guide helps users navigate the NIVAAS platform based on their role. The system has four primary interfaces tailored to different stakeholders.

---

## For Slum Dwellers

### Getting Started

1. **Download/Open** the NIVAAS app (mobile or web)
2. **Register** (optional)—you can submit anonymous surveys
3. **Allow location** access for accurate mapping

### Submitting a Survey

1. Tap **Survey** in the main menu
2. Fill in demographic information (household size, income range, etc.)
3. Answer living condition questions
4. Submit—your data helps improve settlement mapping and welfare access

### Accessing Welfare Schemes

1. Go to **Welfare** section
2. View schemes you may be eligible for based on your survey
3. Tap a scheme for details and application links
4. Follow instructions to apply on government portals

### Emergency Button

- Tap the **Emergency** button for immediate assistance
- Your location will be shared with nodal officers
- Use only in genuine emergencies

---

## For Citizens (Reporters)

### Reporting an Illegal Settlement

1. **Open** NIVAAS (web or mobile)
2. Tap **Report**
3. **Take a photo** of the settlement/encroachment
4. **Confirm location** (GPS auto-detects; you can adjust pin)
5. Add optional **description** (e.g., "New structures near river")
6. **Submit**

### After Submission

- You'll receive a **confirmation** with a reference ID
- You can **track** your report status (optional, if you create an account)
- Nodal officers will verify and update the case

---

## For Nodal Officers

### Logging In

1. Open the **Nodal Officer Dashboard**
2. Enter your **credentials**
3. You'll see your assigned region and case queue

### Managing Cases

1. **View** cases in your queue (New, In Progress, Pending)
2. **Open** a case to see report details, photos, location
3. **Verify** on-ground—visit the location and confirm
4. **Update status**—Verified, Disputed, or Escalate
5. Add **notes** for audit trail

### Heatmap View

- Switch to **Map** view to see settlement density
- Use filters for date range, report type
- Click clusters to drill down

### Emergency Alerts

- **Emergency** alerts appear at the top of your dashboard
- Tap to view location and respond
- Mark as acknowledged when attended

---

## For Government Users

### Dashboard Overview

1. **Login** to Government Dashboard
2. View **summary metrics**—total settlements, reports, trends
3. **Heatmap** shows city-wide settlement distribution
4. **Charts** show trends over time

### Reports

1. Go to **Reports** section
2. Filter by region, date, settlement type
3. **Export** to PDF or Excel for meetings
4. **Share** with stakeholders (future)

### Analytics

- **Demographics**—age, income, household size from surveys
- **Settlement growth**—new reports over time
- **Welfare uptake**—scheme applications from dwellers

## Tips & Best Practices

| Role | Tip |
|------|-----|
| **Dweller** | Complete surveys accurately—it improves welfare eligibility matching |
| **Citizen** | Include clear photos; avoid blurry or irrelevant images |
| **Officer** | Verify within 48 hours; add detailed notes for escalations |
| **Government** | Use filters to focus on high-priority regions |
